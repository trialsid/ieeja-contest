Simple Steps to Share Your Voice

The contest is open to soloists, duos, and groups who feel connected to Ieeja. Follow the three easy steps below and we’ll see you in the spotlight.

1. Join the Lineup

Complete the online form with your contact information, song choice, and a short bio.

2. Record Your Performance

Submit a clear audio or video track (MP3, WAV, or MP4) between two and four minutes long.

3. Send & Confirm

Email your file and proof of registration to sing@ieeja.org. Expect a confirmation within 48 hours.
