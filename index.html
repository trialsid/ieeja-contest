About the Contest

The Ieeja Voices Singing Contest brings together vocalists from our community to celebrate local talent. Record your best performance, send it to us, and inspire the whole town.

Who Can Join?

Anyone with a connection to Ieeja who loves to sing—soloists, duos, and groups are welcome.

Submission Format

Share a high-quality audio or video recording up to 4 minutes long showcasing your vocal skills.

Important Dates

  • Registration Opens: April 15
  • Submission Deadline: May 20
  • Live Showcase: June 2 at Ieeja Town Hall

How to Register

  1. Complete the Form

    Fill out the online registration form with your contact details and song selection.

  2. Prepare Your Recording

    Record a clear performance video or audio clip (MP4, MP3, or WAV) lasting between 2 and 4 minutes.

  3. Submit & Confirm

    Email your recording and proof of registration to sing@ieeja.org. We will send a confirmation within 48 hours.

Judging Criteria

Vocal Quality

Pitch accuracy, tone, and control across your performance.

Expression

Emotion, storytelling, and connection with your audience.

Creativity

Song choice, arrangement, and originality in delivery.

Technical Skill

Breath control, dynamics, and overall musicality.
